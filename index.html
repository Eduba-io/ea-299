01 — The frame

A relationship bank does not scale advisor AI on a tool. It scales it on a methodology.

The right banking partner can help a client define a path. The right methodology partner can help a bank define the path its own advisors will walk through the next five years of AI tooling without losing the craft that makes the relationship valuable.

60%
Database & workflow
Client records, relationship notes, portfolio reviews, meeting prep. The work is to make this layer faster, not smarter.
30%
Rule-based logic
Appropriateness review, Regulation BI checkpoints, supervisory documentation, OCC-aware process.
10%
Genuine AI
Multi-generational transitions, unusual tax structuring, complex family-office work. Where the advisor decides how to lean on the tool, and where to put it down.

Sorting those three into the right layers is the engagement. The curriculum that follows teaches an advisor exactly where AI augments the relationship and exactly where the relationship still carries the work alone.
